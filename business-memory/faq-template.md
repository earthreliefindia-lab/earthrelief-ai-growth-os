# FAQ Master Template

Use this file to store customer questions and approved Earth Relief answers.

## Format

```text
Q: Customer question

A: Approved answer
```

## Example FAQs

Q: What is Earth Relief?

A: Earth Relief is a sustainable products and packaging business focused on eco-friendly, biodegradable, hygienic, and business-friendly solutions.

Q: Do you support bulk orders?

A: Yes. Earth Relief is designed for B2B and bulk buyer requirements. MOQ depends on product category and customization needs.

Q: Is custom branding available?

A: Yes. Logo and branding customization can be supported for eligible bulk orders.

Q: Why should a business switch from plastic packaging?

A: Sustainable packaging can improve brand image, reduce plastic dependency, and help businesses prepare for rising customer and regulatory expectations.

Q: Are all products biodegradable?

A: Product material and biodegradability depend on the specific product category. Always verify the product specification before making a final claim.
