# Earth Relief Sales Playbook

This file stores approved sales responses, objection handling, and conversion-focused messaging.

## Sales Positioning

Earth Relief sells sustainable packaging as a business growth and brand trust solution, not only as an eco-friendly product.

## Common Situations

### Customer says: Price is high

Approved response:

Plastic may look cheaper at first, but packaging affects brand perception, customer trust, and future readiness. Earth Relief helps businesses move toward sustainable packaging while maintaining practical quality.

### Customer says: Send details

Approved response:

Thank you for your interest in Earth Relief. We provide sustainable and biodegradable packaging solutions for businesses and bulk buyers. Please share your product requirement, quantity, branding needs, and delivery location so we can suggest the right option.

### Customer asks: Do you provide custom branding?

Approved response:

Yes, custom logo and branding options may be available for eligible bulk orders. Final feasibility depends on product category, quantity, and print requirements.

### Customer asks: What is MOQ?

Approved response:

MOQ depends on the product type, size, material, and customization requirements. Please share your product requirement and estimated quantity so we can guide you accurately.

## Closing Lines

- Would you like us to prepare a bulk quote for your requirement?
- Please share quantity and delivery location so we can suggest the most suitable option.
- We can help you move from plastic-heavy packaging to a cleaner, brand-friendly alternative.
