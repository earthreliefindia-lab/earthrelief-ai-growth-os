# Postiz Publishing Rules

These rules define how approved Earth Relief content should move into social scheduling.

## Golden Rule

Postiz should receive only approved content.

No AI agent should directly publish content in V1.

## Publishing Status Flow

```text
AI Generated
↓
Pending Approval
↓
Approved
↓
Postiz Draft
↓
Manual Final Review
↓
Scheduled
↓
Published
```

## Platform Rules

### Instagram

Use for:

- Reels
- Short hooks
- Emotional sustainability stories
- Visual transformation content
- Product awareness

Avoid:

- Long technical explanations
- Unsupported environmental claims

### Facebook

Use for:

- Community awareness
- Business education
- Local demand generation
- Shareable sustainability posts

### LinkedIn

Use for:

- B2B authority
- Founder-led posts
- Packaging industry insights
- Sustainability and business strategy
- Bulk buyer education

### YouTube Shorts

Use for:

- Fast awareness videos
- Trend-based storytelling
- Before/after packaging content
- Educational hooks

## Required Before Scheduling

Every post must include:

```text
platform
caption
CTA
risk level
approval status
review date
```

## High-Risk Content Rule

High-risk content must not be scheduled automatically.

Examples:

- legal claims
- government policy claims
- competitor comparisons
- price claims
- biodegradability claims without proof
- health and hygiene claims

## Founder Review Checklist

Before scheduling, confirm:

```text
Is the message on-brand?
Is the CTA clear?
Is the claim safe?
Is the platform format correct?
Does it support Earth Relief's long-term brand equity?
```
